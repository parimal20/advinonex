Sitra Order for IOCL

Advinonex Automation is delighted to announce a new customer milestone – the award of a prestigious project from Sitra, with Indian Oil Corporation Limited (IOCL) as the end customer.

This engagement covers a total of 23 controller locations across multiple critical pipeline projects:
Project 1: 16 locations – HMRPL-CBPL Pipeline Controller requirement
Project 2: 2 locations – Group-A of HRPL-MER_CUF-JGPL Pipeline
Project 3: 5 locations – Group-B of HRPL-MER_CUF-JGPL Pipeline

As part of this project, Advinonex will supply state-of-the-art Brodersen RTU32M Controllers, enabling advanced automation, reliability, and efficiency across IOCL’s pipeline infrastructure.

This milestone further strengthens Advinonex’s commitment to delivering cutting-edge automation solutions for the energy sector and highlights our growing collaboration with strategic industry leaders.

We thank Sitra and IOCL for their trust in Advinonex and look forward to successful execution and long-term partnership.

Siemens SCADA Wireless POC

We recently conducted a Proof of Concept (PoC) test demonstrating Brodersen RTU32M Controller wireless communication with Siemens SP5 SCADA over the IEC-60870-5-104 protocol.

The test was carried out jointly in the presence of the Siemens India team, and we are glad to report that it was a great success.

We sincerely thank the Siemens team for witnessing the demonstration and acknowledging our efforts. This success reinforces our commitment to delivering robust and reliable automation and communication solutions.
