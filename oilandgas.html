At ADVINONEX, we are dedicated to delivering state-of-the-art automation solutions for Oil & Natural Gas Pipeline Automation, ensuring operational efficiency, safety, and reliability across critical pipeline networks.

We specialize in integrating robust automation, monitoring, and control technologies for RCP/MOV stations, Scrubber stations, and attended locations, driving digital transformation in India’s Oil & Gas midstream operations with scalable and secure systems.

Our Pipeline Automation Solutions

  • Remote Terminal Units (RTUs) – Supply, configuration, and commissioning for automation and field data acquisition.
  • SCADA Integration – Seamless connectivity with central SCADA systems for remote monitoring and control.
  • Valve and Scrubber Automation – Advanced remote control for Motor Operated Valves (MOVs), Remote Control Panels (RCPs), and Scrubber stations.
  • Real-Time Monitoring – Tracking of pressure, flow, temperature, valve position, gas detection, and leak detection inputs.
  • Secure Communication Networks – Reliable networks using protocols like Modbus, IEC, and DNP3 for field-to-SCADA connectivity.
  • Safety and Compliance – Configuration of alarms, interlocks, safety shutdowns, and event logs for operational integrity.
